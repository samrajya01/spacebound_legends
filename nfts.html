The 7 Elements are not only NFTs but also the most important components of this story and game. In the story, Lumos and Orion embark on a quest to find these Elements, and as they acquire each Element, the corresponding NFT will become available in the NFT market. These NFTs serve as the key to Season 2 for the entire community. Gold Pass owners will receive these NFT for free (Terms and conditions apply).
